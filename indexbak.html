ABOUT US
HomeX is company that transforms the way people love their homes. We blend modern technology with the core services that make home ownership easy, comfortable and extraordinary. We see ourselves as the dependable everyday heroes that anticipate, resolve and manage ongoing home needs so that our consumers may have the freedom to enjoy what is most important in life, worry free.
MANAGEMENT TEAM
MICHAEL WERNER
FOUNDER & CEO
A long-time entrepreneur and strategist, Michael founded HomeX based on the belief that taking care of your home should be easy and delightful. In addition to leading the Home Experience team, he is the Chairman of the Lymphoma Research Foundation, the world’s largest non-profit focused exclusively on eradicating lymphoma and serving those touched by the disease. From 2002-2016, Michael was the President and CEO of Globe Union Group Inc. and previously, he was President of his family’s business, Werner Ladder Co. He is a Director of the Chicago Council on Global Affairs and serves on several industry and non-profit boards. Michael holds a BS and MA from Stanford University.
STEPHANIE WERNER
CO-FOUNDER, HEAD OF STRATEGY, INTERIM CMO
Stephanie has a background in strategic consulting specializing in innovation, brand and marketing strategy, growth strategy, and consumer experience. Before joining HomeX, Stephanie served as an operating partner in an early-stage CPG incubator. She has also spent several years as a strategy consultant, both independently and at Prophet, a management consulting firm focused on growth, brand, and marketing strategy. Stephanie holds a BA and an MBA from Stanford University.
DENNIS DONOVAN
CO-FOUNDER, VICE-PRESIDENT
Dennis has nearly 20 years of continued and progressive experience in the Home Services, Energy and Financial Services Space. From 1999 to 2012, Dennis worked at Nicor, Inc. in their deregulated energy and home services division serving in several management and strategic roles culminating as a Vice President of Retail Operations. In 2012, Dennis took leadership role at Direct Energy with their Home Services Division, in his last capacity as General Manager & Executive Director of Home Services. In 2017, Dennis joined the HomeX management team as Co-Founder and Vice President where he services as a Principal. Through a graduate fellowship, Dennis earned a M.S. in economics from Illinois State University.
